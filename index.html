About Me

I'm Max Aeon Parks, a researcher and educator at the intersection of Philosophy, Artificial Intelligence, and Human Factors in AI systems. My work explores ethical integration of AI with human cognition and society, emphasizing bias mitigation, emotional intelligence benchmarking, and extended cognition.

Key Research Areas

  • Philosophy of AI: Ethical implications, consciousness, decision-making frameworks, and foundational AI theory.
  • Human Factors in AI: Human-AI interaction, AI emotional intelligence, usability, and cognitive-computational perspectives.
  • Artistic Exploration: Exploring AI-generated art to probe intersections of technology, identity, and human experience.

Educational Initiatives

  • Duck-Rabbit Φ Game: Interactive philosophy game for kids to foster philosophical inquiry and critical thinking.
